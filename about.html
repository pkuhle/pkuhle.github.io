About Me

I'm a senior at Georgia Tech graduating in May, 2024 with my BS in Electrical Engineering. I'll also be staying at Georgia Tech for my MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE), graduating in May, 2025. I chose to study electrical engineering because I take deep satisfaction in understanding the fundamental physics and mathematics that govern the natural world in addition to the applicability and tangibility of engineering.

I'm a proud native of the Pacific Northwest, hailing from a little town just outside of Portland, OR called Camas, WA. I love staying active by hiking and playing tennis, and I'm an avid Seattle Seahawks and Portland Trail Blazers fan. Even though I haven't gotten to play much in college, I'm a classically trained pianist with over 15 years of experience. I also am an active member of a collegiate ministry called Reformed University Fellowship (RUF) and a rocketry club called Georgia Tech Experimental Rocketry (GTXR).

I pride myself on being a well balanced individual, having both strong technical knowledge and interpersonal skills. I'm always asking questions to expand my knowledge and to tackle unfamiliar problems; I'm not afraid to admit what I don't know. I believe that I posses a unique set of skills and qualities that would make me a valuable addition to any workplace environment.
